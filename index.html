Daisuke Akatsuka

都立航空工業高等専門学校在籍中から四輪レーサーを目指すも断念。蕎麦屋、溶接屋を経てプログラマになる。2003年IPA未踏ユース（スーパークリエイタ）、2006年IPA未踏ソフトウェア創造事業採択。2011年慶應義塾大学大学院政策・メディア研究科修了。現在は Birchill に所属し、ウェブブラウザ Firefox の Address Bar および DevTools を中心に開発を行う。2021年設立した zeroinon では自分世代の高齢社会を考えている。

While a student at Tokyo Metropolitan College of Aeronautical Engineering, he gave up his goal of becoming a car racer. He became a programmer after working at a soba noodle shop and a welding shop. Was accepted into 2003 IPA MITOU Youth (Super Creator), 2006 IPA MITOU Software. Was graduated from Graduate School of Media and Governance, Keio University 2011. He is currently a member of Birchill, where he develops mainly Address Bar and DevTools for Firefox web browser. zeroinon, which he founded in 2021, is considering the aging society of his generation.
